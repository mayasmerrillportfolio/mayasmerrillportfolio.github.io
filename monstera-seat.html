Product Design

Monstera
Seat

A bicycle seat inspired by monstera leaves, designed for quicker drying time and less material waste. Modeled with Rhino and Grasshopper using voronoi patterns.

Ideation Sketching

I began by first ideating ways to use parametric design for bike safety by utilizing it for helmets. However, after some 3D modeling, I shifted to using parametric design (specifically voronoi patterning) for creating a quicker-drying bike seat.
