Provinces of Cambodia —
Illustrated Maps

Phnom Penh, Cambodia

While teaching students at AUPP Liger Leadership Academy, I designed and led a project-based course aimed at teaching sixty 7th and 8th grade Cambodian students skills in Adobe Illustrator and InDesign. My co-facilitator, Souyeth Kret, and I guided this project, which involved helping students illustrate and label (in English and Khmer) significant cultural landmarks from all 25 provinces of Cambodia and assemble them into maps following color and font guidelines.

Illustrated and labeled by students at AUPP Liger Leadership Academy. Art Direction by Maya Merrill.
