Product Design

5 Rings
Exhibition

Eugene, Oregon

In Spring 2024, I collaborated with other Product Design students at the University of Oregon to design an exhibition featuring our ring designs inspired by the Track Olympic trials in Eugene. We each produced our ring design in five different materials and manufacturing methods to showcase the different environmental impacts of each method and material. I designed and produced the pictured rings, a design titled 'Feather,' and was the main branding designer.

This exhibition has been featured in the following locations and events: 5th Street Market in Eugene, Oregon (June 2024), Dutch Design Week in Eindhoven (October 18-26 2025) and Greener Manufacturing in Cologne, Germany (Nov. 12-13 2025).
