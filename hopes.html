HOPES[29]
Conference

Eugene, Oregon

Holistic Options for Planet Earth Sustainability, or HOPES, is an annual design conference that invites design professionals from all over the United States to give lectures at the University of Oregon College of Design on topics relating to design and its role in impacting and shaping society and environment. As Director of Branding, I worked with a team of other student directors to develop a branding guide and create outreach materials across digital and physical executions.
