University of Oregon
Branding

Eugene, Oregon

As a graphic designer for the UO Marketing and Communications Department for three years, I regularly collaborated with senior designers to produce digital and printed graphics that promoted university departments, programs and student services for incoming and current UO students. All designs made in Adobe Illustrator to promote the University of Oregon for the UO Marketing & Communications department.

A gif showing the multiple iterations I made throughout the process of creating my November illustration for the UO calendar.

Illustration

I made this concept and illustration in Adobe Illustrator for the official University of Oregon calendar for November 2022. This calendar is distributed to new students and their families each year.

I created this concept and illustration for the 2023 incoming freshman postcard pack that is sent out to each incoming class as a means of promoting the University of Oregon and welcoming new students. It merges a photo of campus with an illustrated world (through the signature "O" hands) that shows the vibrant diversity of activities and students found at UO.

The second design in this "O" series, I illustrated this design for the UO postcards distributed to incoming freshmen.

I helped organize and promote 'THE STUDIO' annual art exhibit. This campus exhibit occurs every spring and features UO students' work. Above are the riso-print illustrations and infographics I created to promote this event through posters and social media.

I created this poster promoting the student sustainability poster on the UO campus using whimsical designs to inform students of resources such as free water bottles for the UO Marketing and Communications department.

I designed and illustrated this three-piece set of 'tarot' cards to represent the diversity of students and activities at the University of Oregon to promote the UO to incoming students.

I illustrated this graphic for the National Hispanic Heritage month for the Financial Wellness Center on campus, inspired by Hispanic art.
