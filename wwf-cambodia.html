Earth Day 2025

As a WWF Earth Day volunteer, I helped film the 2025 Earth Day and Mekong River Swim hosted in Kratie Province, Cambodia by WWF Cambodia. I edited this footage and other footage taken by the WWF Communications teams to create a recap video of WWF Cambodia's largest public engagement events of the year.
